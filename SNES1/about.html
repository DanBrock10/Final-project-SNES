About Super Nintendo

The Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) is a 16-bit home video game console developed by Nintendo. Released in 1990, it has become one of the most iconic gaming systems of all time, known for its high-quality games and exceptional hardware for its era.

The SNES featured some of the most beloved video games, such as Super Mario World, The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past, and Super Metroid. The console's success laid the foundation for the future of gaming, and it continues to be celebrated by fans worldwide.

Since its release, the SNES has been recognized not only for its classic games but also for its innovative design and the way it revolutionized the video game industry.

Our Legacy

At Super Nintendo, we strive to preserve the legacy of the SNES and continue to celebrate the golden age of gaming. Our goal is to offer new generations the chance to experience the magic of the SNES, while also bringing together long-time fans of this incredible system.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the magic of SNES games continues to inspire the gaming community. By sharing its history, we hope to keep the nostalgia alive while embracing the future of gaming technology.
